# Phase 5: Plan 1 — Digital Prescriptions & Doctor Issuance

<objective>
Enable Doctors to securely issue digital prescriptions for a completed 15-minute slot through a dedicated clinical interface.
</objective>

<tasks>
<task>
  <description>Create Prescription Model</description>
  <read_first>
    - src/models/Slot.ts
  </read_first>
  <action>
    Create `src/models/Prescription.ts` with `slotId` (Ref Slot), `patientId` (Ref User), `doctorId` (Ref User), `medicines` (Array of objects: name, dosage, frequency), and `clinicalNotes` (String).
  </action>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - `src/models/Prescription.ts` exists and correctly links to Slot and User.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task>

  <read_first>
    - src/middleware/blockchain-auth.ts
  </read_first>
  <action>
    Create `src/app/api/prescriptions/route.ts`.
    Implement `POST` function that:

    3. Saves the prescription to MongoDB.
  </action>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - `/api/prescriptions` correctly saves digital notes to the database.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task>

  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>
